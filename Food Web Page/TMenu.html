MAGGIE

Ingredients

  1. 50g carrots.
  2. 50g Capsicum.
  3. 30g chickpeas.
  4. diced Onion and Tomato and chillies.
  5. 50g Parmesan cheese, grated.
  6. 2 garlic cloves, minced.
  7. Black pepper, to taste.

Instructions

  1. Boil the noodles in hot water for 2 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan and saute onion then chillies and then tomato.
  3. cook till it becomes like paste.
  4. add chopped veggies and cook
  5. now add the boiled noodles and turn the flame off after 1 min.


HOT CHOCOLATE

Ingredients

  1. Coco powder or chocolate bar
  2. condensed milk
  3. sugar

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk.
  2. Add sugar (as per your convenience).
  3. Stir thoroughly.
  4. Add coco powder or chocolate bar.
  5. cook until all lumps are not visible.
  6. Pour it in cup and enjoy.


CHAI

Ingredients

  1. Tealeaf.
  2. Milk.
  3. Sugar.
  4. Caradom.

Instructions

  1. Boil the milk.
  2. Add sugar (as per your convenience).
  3. Add Tealeafs.
  4. Add caradom and grated ginger(optional).
  5. Strain it in cup, serve with some biscuits or bread and enjoy.
